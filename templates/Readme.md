PROBLEM DEFINITION:
 
The automotive industry is rife with challenges for vehicle owners when it comes to managing their maintenance needs effectively and efficiently. From sourcing genuine spare parts to locating reliable service centers and skilled mechanics, navigating the complexities of automotive maintenance can be a daunting task. Traditional methods often involve manual processes, lack transparency, and are prone to inefficiencies and delays. Moreover, the absence of a centralized platform exacerbates these challenges, making it difficult for vehicle owners to access information, make informed decisions, and procure quality services and parts.

SCOPE FOR FUTURE ENHANCEMENT 

The scope of the AUTOCARE project encompasses the development and implementation of a comprehensive platform that addresses the challenges faced by vehicle owners in managing their automotive maintenance needs. Initially, the focus will be on creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface for users to access information about service centers, browse genuine spare parts, schedule appointments with mechanics, and manage maintenance tasks efficiently. Integration with geolocation APIs will enable users to locate nearby service centers and shops easily, enhancing convenience and accessibility. 
Additionally, the platform will include features for user reviews, ratings, and forums to foster community engagement and enable knowledge sharing within the automotive community.
